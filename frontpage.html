What Are Solar Panels?

Understanding Solar Technology

Solar panels are devices that convert sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic cells. They are made of semiconductor materials that absorb photons from sunlight and release electrons, creating an electric current.

Modern solar panels are highly efficient, durable, and can last 25-30 years with minimal maintenance. They work in various weather conditions and can significantly reduce your electricity bills.

Solar energy is a renewable, clean source of power that helps reduce carbon emissions and environmental impact while providing long-term financial benefits to homeowners.
